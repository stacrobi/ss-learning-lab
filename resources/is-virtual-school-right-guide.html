Is Virtual School Right for Your Child?

Good signs

Your child benefits from flexibility, can work independently with structure, and does better outside a traditional school setting.

Warning signs

Your child needs constant one-to-one instruction, cannot stay engaged without frequent intervention, or lacks a stable daytime environment.

Questions to ask

Who supports the school day, how will routines be managed, and what environment helps your child stay calm and accountable?

Next step
