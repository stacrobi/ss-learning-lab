Daily Schedule Template for Kids

Morning launchWake up, breakfast, get dressed, and set up the learning space.
First academic blockAttend virtual classes and complete priority assignments.
Movement breakFive to ten minutes for water, stretching, and reset.
Second academic blockIndependent work with a visible checklist.
LunchOffline time, meal, and a social or calming reset.
Afternoon work blockFinish remaining school tasks and review missed items.
Pack-up routineCharge device, organize materials, and note tomorrow's priorities.
Family check-inReview what went well and where support is still needed.
